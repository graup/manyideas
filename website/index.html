Many Ideas

Inclusive communities collecting issues and generating ideas

Many Ideas is a system in which members of a community engage with each other to collect issues, generate ideas, and discuss matters of concern. Officials pledge to respond to community issues. Together, we can make our environments a better place!

The system is designed to be inclusive and collect diverse opinions by employing techniques from recent findings of social computing research.

If you’re interested in our research or want to deploy an instance for your community, don’t hesitate to get in touch.

Design backed by scientific research

1. Creating a positive environment

2. Defining and achieving real impact

3. Personalized motivation support

Everyone is motivated in different ways, but most applications are built with a certain population in mind.
